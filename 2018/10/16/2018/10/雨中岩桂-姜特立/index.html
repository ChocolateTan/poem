雨中岩桂-姜特立

社后十日雨，坏却山桂花。|不见日车行，但见雨脚斜。|闷坐室何有，辞眠情不佳。|安得万里风，浩荡吹晴霞。
Ten days after the rain, bad mountain osmanthus. I do not see the daily bus, but I see the rain is slanting. There is no room for sleep. The wind is blowing, and the wind is blowing away.
